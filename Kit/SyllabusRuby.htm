Course Overview

This course will teach you the Ruby programming language. We will draw on the Software Inventions Ruby Examples, and on recorded lectures (supplied as part of your materials fee). We'll start with 3 hours of in-person lecture, followed by 7 hours of online lecture/meetings during the break. The online lectures will consist of recorded Flash lectures of around 40 min each, followed by a 15 min online meeting for questions and discussion via Elluminate. Links for the Elluminate meetings will be sent to you via Email. You must attend at least 4 of the Elluminate meetings unless otherwise arranged with me. You may skip 3 of them, provided you have reviewed and completely understood the lecture on which the meeting is based before the meeting occurs, and have confirmed this to me via an Email. (Sample content of such Email: “Clint, I plan to skip the <date/time> Elluminate meeting as one of my allowed 3 absences. I have already reviewed the lecture on <subject> and I completely understand all points in it.”)



Online Homework

You'll do four modest (20-60 lines each, in my implementations) Ruiby programs, and a quite-challenging exercise on regular expressions. These will be submitted and automatically graded online via IHS. All of these must be completed successfully to pass the class.



Tests

The course will have one 1-hour final exam, based very heavily on the homework and projects. This must be taken in person, and will be proctored by my TA, Doug Gallatin, from 5:30-6:30 pm in 14-301.



Noncollaboration Policy

Collaboration on code and test generation is strictly forbidden in this course.  Each individual in the class is to do his/her own work on the programming projects, and homework projects.  Violation of this standard results in an automatic F in the course, plus additional penalties. Attached to this syllabus is an agreement to sign indicating that you understand and will adhere to this policy.  This agreement must be returned to me before you can purchase an enrollment key for the online workbook.
